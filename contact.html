FAQ

how to cancel booking?

You can cancel your booking from the “My Bookings” section in your dashboard. Select the property and click on the cancel option. Charges may apply as per policy.

when will I get the possession?

The possession date depends on the project completion timeline. Once the construction is complete and all approvals are cleared, we will notify you via email and SMS regarding handover.

where can I pay the rent?

You can pay your rent securely through our online portal using debit/credit cards, net banking, or UPI. Offline options such as cheque or cash payments at the office are also available.

how to contact with the buyers?

Registered buyers can be contacted directly through the in-app chat or by requesting a call back. Your contact details remain protected until you confirm communication.

why my listing not showing up?

Your listing may be under review for verification. Please ensure all details are correct, images are clear, and documents are submitted. It usually takes 24–48 hours to go live.

how to promote my listing?

You can promote your listing by choosing a premium plan, which highlights your property at the top of search results. Featured ads and social media promotions are also available.
